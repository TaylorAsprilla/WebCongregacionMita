Childhood and Youth

The Counselor (Bible School)

Founded in 1956

It was established so that children receive spiritual guidance, Bible classes, advice on good behavior and notions about social and civic responsibilities. The teachers of The Counselor are faithful brothers, of good testimony, with extensive knowledge of the Bible and of the doctrine of our Congregation.

Youth

The Mita Congregation does an extraordinary job with the thousands of young people who profess our faith. Religious services held on Tuesdays are dedicated to young people. They are gathered periodically exhorting them to their spiritual, moral and social improvement. At these meetings, They discuss topics of interest. In addition, recreational and sports activities are carried out with the purpose of cultivating a healthy coexistence. Art is also encouraged among our youth in all its facets. Concerts, recitals, Biblical expositions, sports tournaments and cultural excursions that keep them united and productive in positive aspects.
