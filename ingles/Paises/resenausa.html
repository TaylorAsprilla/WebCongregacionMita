USA

The Church of Mita arrived in the United States in 1948. Some brothers who had joined the congregation in Puerto Rico, had moved to New York City in search of progress.

Given this situation, The Person of Mita sends the first preacher, Domingo Hernández, to the great city to bring this message of Love, Freedom and Unity.

Later, the church extended to several cities on the east coast such as Connecticut, New Jersey and Washington D.C. In the 60s, it arrives in Miami and Chicago.

Since 1970, a great expansion begins throughout the American nation. The church arrives in Orlando and Tampa, Houston and Dallas, Boston and Sprinfield in the state of Massachusetts, Long Branch, Long Island, Maryland, Ohio, North Carolina, Alaska, among others.

Moreover, the Congregation has about nine musical bands in the United States, which have stood out in different events of the US and Canadian government, in addition to its presentations in the annual parade of our congregation held in Puerto Rico


HEALTH TESTIMONIES IN THE US

An extremely amazing testimony occurred to Sister María Pérez González. Our sister was suffering from cancer in the abdominal area. In October 1984, Maria entered into a coma.

One of her daughters called the headquarters in Hato Rey so that Aaron pray to her. Aaron moved immediately to Ponce, where the sister was, and when he saw her dying, he was touched. The Holy Spirit, through Aaron, said: «Maria, Maria, Maria, wake up. This is The Father". The sister woke up from that lethargy instantly. Aaron told her that he was going to send her to New York City, and he bought the ticket himself.

Before leaving, he gave her these instructions: "Go to the Hudson River and throw that disease there, under the snow."

Upon arriving in New York, Maria went to the Hudson River, took a stone, wrote the name of Mita with faith, and threw it into the river, exclaiming that the disease came out of her. That same night snowed, and the sister was totally healthy. After this health experience, María Pérez lived 33 more years. She continued in her preaching ministry announcing this message of salvation in Puerto Rico and many other countries.

Another testimony that we want to share was the one that occurred to Sister Loyda Quiles, in 2018.

Our sister faced a very delicate health situation. She had formed HER2+ type nodules which meant they were carcinogens. She was very worried about this situation, but with faith she called Rosinín and informed her of the situation. Rosinín prayed and with the power of the Holy Spirit said: "Let those nodules reduce to their minimum!"

The word of the prophet had its faithful fulfillment, and those nodules were reduced and disappeared from her body. The doctors had to accept that those nodules had disappeared inexplicably.
