Historical Overview

Birth of Juanita Garcia Peraza

June 24, 1897

Birth of Aarón

October 23, 1921

Birth of Rosinín

October 18, 1937

The Mita Congregation is founded in Arecibo, Puerto Rico with 11 brothers.

1940

Inauguration of the first temple located in the Las Monjas sector in San Juan, Puerto Rico.

January 11, 1943

The headquarters of the Congregation is moved from Arecibo to San Juan, Puerto Rico. The Corps of Guards is established.

1947

The Mita Congregation arrives in New York.

1948

The String Ensemble is established.

1950

The El Paraíso Farm is acquired.

1951

The Mita Band was established.

1956

Rosinín joins the Church of Mita.

1962

The Mita Congregation arrives in the Dominican Republic.

1963

The Mita Band participates in the Puerto Rican Parade in New York City.

1969

Passing of Juanita García Peraza and Aarón assumes the leadership of the Congregation. The Church of Mita arrives in Colombia.

1970

The Hermanos Unidos Savings and Credit Union is founded.

1974

The Bayamón farm is acquired.

1976

The Mita Congregation arrives in Mexico.

1980

Mita Congregation School is founded.

1981

The Mita Congregation arrives in Venezuela.

1982

The Égida e Instutición El Paraíso (Shelter House) is established. The Office of Guidance and Social Work is established.

1985

The Mita Congregation arrives in Costa Rica. The Harp Ensemble is established. The Crafts and Mold Pieces Fair begins.

1986

The Mita Congregation arrives in Panama.

1987

The Mita Band wins the President Award at a band competition in San Juan, Puerto Rico.

1988

Celebration of the fiftieth anniversary of the Mita Congregation and inauguration of the New Temple. Los Hermanos Corporation is established. America Bakery and Pastry is established. The Mita Choir is organized.

1990

The Mita Congregation arrives in Canada.

1991

The Band of Mita is invited by the government of Puerto Rico to participate in the commemoration of the 500 years of the Discovery of The Americas in Seville, Spain (Expo-92).

1992

The Mita Congregation arrives in El Salvador.

1993

The Mita Congregation arrives in Spain.

2003

Los Hermanos Memorial Park is inaugurated.

2010

Aaron names Rosinin "Spiritual Mother".

2012

The Mita Band participates in a parade in the Magic Kingdom park at Walt Disney World.

2018

Passing of Aarón and Rosinín assumes the leadership of the Congregation.
