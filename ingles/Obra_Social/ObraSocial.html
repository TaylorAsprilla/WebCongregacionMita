Mita Congregation School

Founded in 1981

The Mita Congregation School is accredited by the Middle State Association. It offers services of nursery school and teaches from pre-kindergarten to twelfth grade. At the top level It has the commercial and advanced education program. It has received awards nationally and internationally for its sports program, as well as in speech contests, intellectual’s competitions, and talents of the Honor Society, among others. Rosinín, inaugurated the new nursery that will soon open in our school. We thank Rosinín, the members of the Board of Directors, administration, faculty and collaborators for making this dream come true.

Égida e Institución El Paraíso (Shelter House)

Founded in 1985

The Égida e Institución El Paraíso (Shelter House) was founded under the laws established by the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico. As a non-profit institution, it emerges out of the great need to provide specialized services to elderly people who lack care. It has nursing service, social work, spiritual counseling, among others.

Orientation and Social Work Office

Founded in 1985

The Office of Orientation and Social Work of the Mita Congregation has been offering services directly to the community in general (children, adolescents, adults and the elderly) through the case work. It emerges as a result of the great need to achieve the integral development of the individual in our town, since we understand that the human being is a bio-psycho-social-cultural-spiritual entity. It has collegiate and licensed social workers, a clinical psychologist, a therapist education and a division of the Historical Archive, where the entire history of the Congregation is preserved. The services offered to the whole community are free.

Los Hermanos Memorial Park, Inc

Founded in 2010

Los Hermanos Memorial Park, Inc. is an institution of Congregation Mita, Inc. The purpose of the Congregation, through the operation of this cemetery, is to offer its parishioners the opportunity to receive a Christian burial within the biblical precepts. It has all the permissions governmental for its operation and has ample parking, a chapel for religious services, garden areas, niches and tombs.
